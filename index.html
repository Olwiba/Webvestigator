Welcome to Webvestigator!

This tool is easy!

How to :

  • 1. Enter in Website domain or IP Address that you wish to lookup into the search bar.

  • (If you dont have one in mind, leave it blank and it will search for you!)

  • 2. Press the search button.

  • 3. Browse all the information that could be gathered about the target.

Note:

  • - Location may not be exact.

  • - Please remove any excess paths from your domain

    (E.g http://example.com/remove/this/path)

  • - Because of the API limitations only 10,000 calls may be made every hour.
